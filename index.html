Welcome

Did you know more and more British farmers are now selling some, or all, of the food they lovingly produce straight from their farms?
You can find hundreds of producers on our website offering meat, milk, dairy products, poultry, eggs, fruit and veg, honey, preserves and non-food products.
Visit a farm to collect, or order online for delivery to your home. Get searching for farms near you now!

How we work?

We don't sell food here - we signpost you straight to the farms that produce it. We help you get closer to the people, places and animals that produce the food you buy and give you the chance to directly support local farmers.
On this website you can use our Interactive Map to find producers all over the UK offering all kinds of food, view Producer Profiles to learn more about our farmers and visit our Search Products page, to find the food you love.
